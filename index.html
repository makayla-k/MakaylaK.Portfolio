What 2022 Has Taught Me

Friday January 14th, 2023
This past May I graduated with my Bachelors in Computer Science! It was quite a journey to make it to gradution. Back in 2019 I joined a Computer Science cohort program that basically took over my life for three years. I was taking an average of 18 units a semester, I was cross enrolled between a community college and a CSU, every summer and winter I took classes, I participated in extracurriculars that were apart of the cohorts requirements, and to prepare myself for life after college I did everything I could to make myself a well rounded engineer.

About Me

Hello my name is Makayla and I am a Software Engineer!

I am starting this blog to share my story. What life was like during college majoring in a male dominated field, how I stayed motivated after college even after graduating with no job leads, and all the things I have learned along the way. It's been quite a journey but I hope by sharing my experiences I will be able to uplift others!

Currently, I now work for a Startup company as a Customer Success Engineer! I have also worked for Meta and an amazing non-profit CodePath. During school I did everything I could to challenge myself and make myself a well rounded engineer. Now I am continue to better myself not only as a SWE but as an individual as well!

Ethnic Background

I was raised on the island of O'ahu and moved to the mainland at the start of my high school career. Deeply imersed in my Hawaiian culture I learned to dance hula and paddle outrigger canoe with my family's club, Ke Kai O'uhane. One of my passions is to share and perpetuate the aloha (love) to all. My cultural background has taught me to be appreciative, respectful, and most of all creative. I have found this to be an amazing outlet to connect with others.
